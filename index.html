About Us

Evo Strength + Fitness is a strength and performance gym with a goal to provide for the needs of strength athletes and general fitness enthusiasts in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

We pride ourselves in providing the highest quality equipment and facilities for powerlifters and individuals looking to improve their lives through strength. Whether you are preparing for your next powerlifting meet and need to train on competition spec equipment or are just stepping foot into the gym for the first time,
Evo Strength + Fitness has you covered.

Stop by and see what we have to offer.
